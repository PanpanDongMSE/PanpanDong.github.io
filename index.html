About me

Panpan received her B.S. from TianGong University (TGU) in 2012 (advisor: Prof. Qingsong Zhang), her M.S. from Tsinghua University (THU) in 2015 (advisor: Prof. Shixi Zhao), and Ph.D. from Washington State University (WSU) in 2020 (advisor: Prof. Min-Kyu Song).

Her research topic mainly focuses on the next-generation lithium batteries with high-energy density, including (1) cathodes design, (2) electrolytes engineering, (3) lithium metal protection, and (4) solid-state electrolytes.

She was the TA for two courses: (1) MSE201 (Materials Science) and (2) MSE302 (Electronic Materials). She also has some teaching experiences of MSE201.
