About me

Panpan is currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Prof. Min-Kyu Song's group . Dr. Dong received her B.S. from TianGong University in 2011, her M.S. from Tsinghua University (THU) in 2015, and Ph.D. from WSU in 2020.

Her research topic mainly focuses on the next-generation lithium batteries with high-energy density, including (1) cathodes design, (2) electrolytes engineering, (3) lithium metal protection, and (4) solid-state electrolytes.

She was the TA for two courses: MSE201 (Materials Science) and MSE302 (Electronic Materials). She also has some teaching experiences of MSE201.
