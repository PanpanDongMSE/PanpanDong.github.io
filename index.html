Panpan Dong, Ph.D.

Postdoctoral Research Associate
School of Mechanical and Materials Engineering
Washington State University
Pullman, WA 99164, U.S.A.

Her research topic mainly focuses on the next-generation lithium batteries with high-energy density, including (1) cathodes design, (2) electrolytes engineering, (3) lithium metal protection, (4) silicon-based anodes, and (5) solid-state electrolytes.
