Education

    • Research Interest

  • Develop and design functional materials for high-energy batteries such as Li/S and Li/Se batteries.
  • Electrolyte engineering for next-generation batteries such as lithium metal protection.
  • Solid-state electrolytes such as polymer electrolytes and MOF-based electrolytes.
  • Functional binders for high-performance electrodes.
