Panpan Dong, Ph.D. in Materials Science and Engineering

Postdoctoral Research Associate
School of Mechanical and Materials Engineering
Washington State University

Her research topic mainly focuses on the next-generation lithium batteries with high-energy density, including (1) cathodes design, (2) electrolytes engineering, (3) lithium metal protection, and (4) solid-state electrolytes.
