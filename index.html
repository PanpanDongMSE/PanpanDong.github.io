About me

I'm currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Washington State University. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Dec. 2020, under supervision of Prof. Min-Kyu Song .

My research topic mainly focuses on the next-generation lithium batteries with high-energy density, including lithium-metal batteries, advanced liquid electrolytes, and solid-state electrolytes.
